Complete tax compliance guide for South African businesses, from SMEs to large corporations. Master corporate tax, VAT, PAYE, and advanced compliance with Taxfy's enterprise-grade platform.

## Business Entity Types & Tax Implications

### 1. Private Companies (Pty Ltd)

#### Tax Obligations

- **Corporate Income Tax**: 27% on taxable income
- **Dividends Tax**: 20% on distributions to shareholders
- **VAT Registration**: Mandatory if turnover > R1 million
- **PAYE**: For employee remuneration
- **UIF**: Employee and employer contributions

#### Key Benefits

- **Limited Liability**: Personal asset protection
- **Tax Planning**: Flexible distribution strategies
- **Credibility**: Enhanced business reputation
- **Perpetual Existence**: Continuous operation

### 2. Close Corporations (CC)

#### Tax Treatment

- **Member's Drawings**: Taxed as deemed remuneration
- **Profits**: Distributed as deemed dividends
- **Corporate Tax**: 27% on retained profits
- **Personal Tax**: On member's share of profits

#### Special Considerations

- **Deemed Remuneration**: R150,000 minimum per active member
- **PAYE**: Required on deemed remuneration
- **UIF**: Contributions required
- **Simplified Returns**: CC Annual Return process

### 3. Sole Proprietorships

#### Tax Structure

- **Personal Income Tax**: Individual rates apply
- **Business Income**: Included in personal assessment
- **VAT**: Registration if turnover > R1 million
- **Provisional Tax**: If owing > R1,000

#### Business Deductions

- **All Qualifying Expenses**: Home office, equipment, travel
- **Retirement Contributions**: Up to 27.5% of income
- **Medical Contributions**: Tax credits available

## Corporate Income Tax Deep Dive

### Tax Calculation Formula

```
Gross Income
- Exempt Income
- Deductions
- Assessed Losses (if any)
= Taxable Income × 27% = Primary Tax

Primary Tax
- Rebates & Credits
- Foreign Tax Credits
= Normal Tax Payable
```

### Special Business Incentives

#### Small Business Corporation (SBC)

**Qualification Criteria**:

- Gross Income < R20 million
- Active Business Income < R20 million
- Shareholders: Natural persons/other SBCs
- Maximum 20% investment income

**Tax Rates for SBCs**:

- R0 - R91,250: 0%
- R91,251 - R365,000: 7%
- R365,001 - R550,000: 21%
- R550,001+: 27%

#### Manufacturing Incentives

## VAT for Business

### Registration Requirements

#### Mandatory Registration

- **Turnover > R1,000,000**: 12-month period
- **Expected Turnover**: Next 12 months projection
- **Registration Timeline**: Within 21 days of threshold

#### Voluntary Registration

- **Turnover > R50,000**: Can register voluntarily
- **Benefits**: Claim input VAT on expenses
- **Export Benefits**: Zero-rate exports

### VAT Return Periods

#### Standard Returns

- **Monthly**: Turnover > R30 million
- **Bi-Monthly**: R1.5 million - R30 million
- **Quarterly**: < R1.5 million (on application)

#### Submission Deadlines

- **Monthly**: 25th of following month
- **Bi-Monthly**: 25th of month after period
- **Payment Due**: Same date as submission

### VAT Calculation & Management

#### Basic Formula

```
Output VAT (Sales) 15%
- Input VAT (Purchases) 15%
= VAT Payable/(Refund)
```

#### Zero-Rated Supplies

- **Exports**: International sales
- **Farming**: Basic foodstuffs
- **Public Transport**: Specific services
- **International Transport**: Cross-border

#### Exempt Supplies

- **Financial Services**: Banking, insurance
- **Medical Services**: Healthcare
- **Educational Services**: Formal education
- **Rental Income**: Residential property

## PAYE Administration

### Employee Tax Obligations

#### Registration Requirements

- **PAYE Number**: Register before first payment
- **UIF Registration**: Unemployment Insurance Fund
- **SDL Registration**: Skills Development Levy
- **COIDA**: Compensation for Occupational Injuries

#### Monthly Obligations

- **PAYE Deduction**: Employee income tax
- **UIF Contributions**: 1% employee + 1% employer
- **SDL Contributions**: 1% of payroll (if > R500,000)
- **EMP201 Return**: Monthly submission by 7th

### Payroll Tax Calculations

#### PAYE Calculation

```
Gross Remuneration
- Retirement Fund Contributions
- Travel Allowance (portion)
= Taxable Income

Apply Tax Tables → PAYE Amount
```

#### UIF Contributions

- **Employee**: 1% of gross (max R177.12/month)
- **Employer**: 1% of gross (max R177.12/month)
- **Maximum Annual**: R2,125.44 per person

#### Skills Development Levy

- **Rate**: 1% of total payroll
- **Threshold**: Annual payroll > R500,000
- **Allowances**: 15% training spend offset

### Annual Compliance

#### IRP5/IT3(a) Certificates

- **Issue Date**: By 31 March following tax year
- **Employee Copies**: Provide within deadline
- **SARS Submission**: Electronic filing required
- **Reconciliation**: Annual reconciliation return

#### Employment Tax Incentive (ETI)

- **Youth Employment**: 18-29 years old
- **Monthly Benefit**: Up to R1,000 per employee
- **Salary Threshold**: R6,500 per month maximum
- **Certification**: ETI certificates required
