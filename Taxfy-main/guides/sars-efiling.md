## Introduction to SARS eFiling

### What is SARS eFiling?

SARS eFiling is the South African Revenue Service's online platform for tax administration. It allows taxpayers to:

- Submit tax returns electronically
- Make payments online
- View tax accounts and statements
- Correspond with SARS digitally
- Access tax certificates and documents

### Benefits of eFiling

- **24/7 Availability**: File anytime, anywhere
- **Faster Processing**: Quicker assessments and refunds
- **Immediate Confirmation**: Electronic acknowledgments
- **Secure Platform**: Bank-grade security
- **Environmental Friendly**: Paperless process
- **Cost Effective**: No printing or postage costs

## eFiling Registration Process

### Individual Registration

#### Requirements

- **South African ID Number** or **Foreign Passport**
- **Valid Email Address** (primary communication method)
- **Cell Phone Number** (for SMS notifications)
- **Banking Details** (for refund payments)

#### Step-by-Step Registration

1. **Visit SARS eFiling Portal**: www.sarsefiling.co.za
2. **Click "Register"**: Select individual registration
3. **Enter Personal Details**: ID number, names, contact details
4. **Verify Information**: Confirm accuracy of all details
5. **Create Password**: Strong password requirements
6. **Email Verification**: Check email for verification link
7. **Complete Registration**: Log in with new credentials

#### Post-Registration Setup

- **Profile Completion**: Add banking and contact details
- **Tax Years**: Activate relevant tax years
- **Representatives**: Authorize tax practitioners if needed
- **Notifications**: Set up SMS and email preferences

### Business Registration

#### Entity Types

- **Companies**: Private companies (Pty Ltd)
- **Close Corporations**: CC registration
- **Trusts**: Various trust types
- **Partnerships**: Business partnerships
- **Sole Proprietors**: Individual business registration

#### Required Information

- **Entity Registration Number**: Company/CC/Trust number
- **Tax Reference Numbers**: Income tax, VAT, PAYE numbers
- **Authorized Representatives**: Directors, members, trustees
- **Business Address**: Physical and postal addresses
- **Banking Details**: Business bank account information

#### Registration Process

1. **Entity Type Selection**: Choose appropriate business type
2. **Registration Details**: Enter all required information
3. **Representative Authorization**: Designate authorized users
4. **Verification Process**: SARS validates information
5. **Account Activation**: Receive activation notification

## Filing Individual Tax Returns (ITR12)

### When to File ITR12

#### Mandatory Filing

- **Gross Income > R500,000** per annum
- **Taxable Capital Gains** during the year
- **Foreign Income**: Any amount
- **Provisional Tax**: Required to pay
- **Director or Partner**: Business involvement

#### Voluntary Filing

- **PAYE Employment**: To claim additional deductions
- **Refund Due**: Expecting tax refund
- **Medical Credits**: Claim additional credits
- **Travel Allowances**: Claim business travel

### ITR12 Sections Explained

#### Section 1: Personal Information

- Taxpayer details (pre-populated)
- Contact information updates
- Residential status (resident/non-resident)
- Tax year period covered

#### Section 2: Income from Employment

- **IRP5 Data**: Salary, wages, allowances
- **Director Remuneration**: Company payments
- **Lump Sum Benefits**: Retirement payouts
- **Foreign Employment**: Offshore income

#### Section 3: Investment Income

- **Interest**: Local and foreign
- **Dividends**: Local and foreign
- **Rental Income**: Property income
- **Capital Gains**: Asset disposals

#### Section 4: Business Income

- **Sole Proprietor**: Business profits
- **Partnership**: Share of partnership income
- **Professional Practice**: Professional fees
- **Farming**: Agricultural income

#### Section 5: Deductions

- **Retirement Contributions**: Pension, provident, RA
- **Medical Expenses**: Qualifying medical costs
- **Travel Expenses**: Business travel claims
- **Professional Expenses**: Work-related costs

#### Section 6: Tax Credits

- **Medical Credits**: Medical scheme fees
- **Foreign Tax Credits**: Tax paid abroad
- **Other Credits**: Special circumstances

## Filing Business Tax Returns

### Company Income Tax Return (ITR14)

#### When to Submit

- **Deadline**: 12 months after financial year-end
- **Extension**: Can apply for extension
- **Provisional Companies**: Different deadlines apply

#### Key Sections

- **Financial Information**: Income statement data
- **Tax Calculation**: Taxable income computation
- **Assessed Losses**: Previous year losses
- **Dividends**: Distributions to shareholders
- **Foreign Income**: International transactions

### VAT Returns (VAT201)

#### Submission Frequencies

- **Monthly**: > R30 million turnover
- **Bi-Monthly**: R1.5m - R30m turnover
- **Quarterly**: < R1.5m (special permission)

#### VAT201 Sections

- **Standard Supplies**: 15% VAT sales
- **Zero-Rated Supplies**: 0% VAT sales
- **Exempt Supplies**: VAT-free sales
- **Input Tax**: VAT on purchases
- **Adjustments**: Prior period corrections

#### Using Taxfy for VAT Returns

1. **Connect Bank Accounts**: Import transactions
2. **Categorize Expenses**: Automatic classification
3. **VAT Extraction**: AI identifies VAT amounts
4. **Generate VAT201**: Export eFiling format
5. **Submit Return**: Upload to SARS eFiling

### PAYE Returns (EMP201)

#### Monthly Obligations

- **Deadline**: 7th of following month
- **Late Penalty**: R250 per month
- **Interest**: 20% per annum on outstanding amounts

#### EMP201 Components

- **PAYE**: Employee income tax deductions
- **UIF**: Unemployment insurance contributions
- **SDL**: Skills development levy
- **Employee Numbers**: Headcount information

## Payment Methods on eFiling

### Electronic Payment Options

#### EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer)

- **Most Popular**: Widely used method
- **Processing Time**: Same day if before 2 PM
- **Bank Requirements**: Internet banking access
- **Reference Numbers**: SARS-generated references

#### Credit Card Payments

- **Convenience**: Immediate processing
- **Cards Accepted**: Visa, MasterCard
- **Fees**: Bank charges may apply
- **Limits**: Check with card issuer

#### Debit Orders

- **Automation**: Set up recurring payments
- **Reliability**: Automatic deduction
- **Management**: Can cancel or modify
- **Suitable For**: Regular obligations (PAYE, VAT)

#### Cash Payments

- **SARS Branches**: Limited branch network
- **Banks**: Selected bank branches
- **Retail Partners**: Pick n Pay, Checkers Money Market
- **Processing**: May take 1-2 business days

### Payment References

- **PRN (Payment Reference Number)**: Unique payment identifier
- **Generation**: Created when payment is initiated
- **Validity**: Usually valid for 7 days
- **Tracking**: Use to verify payment status

## Common eFiling Issues & Solutions

### Login Problems

#### Forgotten Password

1. **Reset Option**: Use "Forgot Password" link
2. **Email Reset**: Check email for reset instructions
3. **Security Questions**: Answer verification questions
4. **New Password**: Create strong new password
5. **Login**: Test new credentials

#### Account Lockout

- **Cause**: Multiple failed login attempts
- **Wait Period**: 30 minutes automatic unlock
- **Contact SARS**: If persistent issues
- **Alternative**: Use SARS branch assistance
