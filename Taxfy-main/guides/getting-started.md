## Understanding Your Results

### Tax Calculation Breakdown

Your results show:

1. **Taxable Income**: Gross income minus deductions
2. **Income Tax**: Based on SARS tax brackets
3. **Tax Rebates**: Primary, secondary, tertiary rebates
4. **Medical Credits**: Tax credits for medical aid
5. **Total Tax**: Final tax liability
6. **Refund/Owing**: Amount due to/from SARS

## Common Tax Deductions

### Retirement Contributions

- **Limit**: 27.5% of gross income or R350,000
- **Types**: Pension, provident, retirement annuity funds
- **Tax Benefit**: Reduces taxable income

### Medical Scheme Contributions

- **Not Deductible**: Provides tax credits instead
- **Credits**: R364 main member, R364 first dependent, R246 additional
- **Benefit**: Direct reduction in tax owed

### UIF Contributions

- **Rate**: 1% of gross income
- **Maximum**: R2,125.44 per year
- **Deductible**: Reduces taxable income

## Troubleshooting

### PDF Upload Issues

**Problem**: PDF won't upload or process
**Solutions**:

- Ensure file is under 10MB
- Check PDF isn't password protected
- Try converting scanned image to text PDF
- Use manual entry as backup

### Incorrect Data Extraction

**Problem**: Extracted values look wrong
**Solutions**:

- Click "Edit" to manually correct values
- Check original IRP5 for accuracy
- Use confidence score as guide
- Verify employer details match

### Calculation Discrepancies

**Problem**: Results don't match expectations
**Solutions**:

- Review all input values
- Check tax year (2025/26 vs previous)
- Verify deduction limits
- Consider age-based rebates
