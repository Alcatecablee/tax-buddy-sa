## Understanding Freelancer Tax Obligations

### Tax Registration Requirements

#### When You Must Register

- **Annual Income > R1,000,000**: Mandatory VAT registration
- **Voluntary VAT**: Register if income > R50,000 annually
- **Income Tax**: All freelancers must register for income tax
- **Provisional Tax**: Required if owing > R1,000 in previous year

#### Required Tax Numbers

- **Tax Reference Number**: Personal income tax
- **VAT Number**: If registered for VAT
- **PAYE Number**: If employing staff
- **UIF Number**: For employee contributions

### Types of Tax Documents for Freelancers

#### IRP5 vs Other Documents

- **IRP5**: If you have employment income
- **IRP5A**: For directors and high-earning freelancers
- **IT3(a)**: For investment income
- **Invoices & Receipts**: For freelance income tracking

## Essential Business Deductions for Freelancers

### Home Office Expenses

#### Qualifying Expenses

- **Rent/Bond Portion**: Based on office space percentage
- **Utilities**: Electricity, water, internet (proportional)
- **Insurance**: Home insurance portion for office
- **Maintenance**: Repairs and upkeep (proportional)

#### Calculation Method

```
Office Size ÷ Total Home Size × Total Expense = Deductible Amount
```

**Example**: 20m² office in 100m² home = 20% of qualifying expenses

### Equipment & Technology

#### Fully Deductible

- **Computers & laptops** (business use only)
- **Software subscriptions** (Adobe, Microsoft, etc.)
- **Professional tools** specific to your trade
- **Smartphones** (if primarily for business)

#### Depreciation Rules

- **Expensive Equipment**: Depreciate over useful life
- **Under R7,000**: Can deduct immediately
- **Mixed Use**: Claim business percentage only

### Travel & Transportation

- **Business Kilometers**: R3.98 per km (2025/26 rate)
- **Actual Costs**: Fuel, maintenance, insurance (business %)
- **Parking & Tolls**: For business travel
- **Vehicle Finance**: Interest portion (business use)

#### Travel Documentation

- **Logbook**: Essential for business km claims
- **Receipts**: All vehicle-related expenses
- **Purpose Records**: Why each trip was necessary

## Provisional Tax for Freelancers

### Who Pays Provisional Tax

- Freelancers owing > R1,000 in the previous year
- Income not subject to PAYE deductions
- Business owners and consultants

### Payment Dates 2025/26

- **First Payment**: 31 August 2025
- **Second Payment**: 28 February 2026
- **Late Payment Penalty**: 20% interest per annum

### Calculation Method

#### Basic Formula

```
Previous Year Tax Liability × 90% = Minimum Payment
OR
Current Year Estimate = Better Option
```

## VAT for Freelancers

### VAT Registration Thresholds

- **Mandatory**: R1,000,000+ annual income
- **Voluntary**: R50,000+ annual income
- **Benefits**: Claim VAT on business expenses
- **Obligations**: Charge 15% VAT on services

### VAT Returns

- **Monthly**: If turnover > R30 million
- **Bi-Monthly**: Standard for most freelancers
- **Submission Deadline**: 25th of month after period

### VAT Calculation

```
Output VAT (charged to clients)
- Input VAT (paid on expenses)
= VAT Owing/Refund
```
